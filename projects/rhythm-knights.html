Rhythm Knights

What I worked on:

  • Graphics
  • Programming
  • Menus
  • Sound

A prototype for MiniJam 125. Written using C# on the Godot Engine. Rhythm Knights is a concept based around planning a turn-based game so that your characters play individual tracks, with the whole party playing a song and scoring bonus points for harmonies. In this prototype, you can only play a few rhythm tracks. It can be played in-browser here (Desktop Only).
