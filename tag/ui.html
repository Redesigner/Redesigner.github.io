Godot Cannon Physics

What I worked on:

  • Design
  • Physics
  • UI

Custom car physics extension for Godot 4.x, written for use in an unspecified game. Features a few sliders so you can play with the physics settings and a test area to drive around in. Uses Jolt Physics for collision and force solving, but custom math for the car parts.
